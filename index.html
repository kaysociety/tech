Girls in ICT Day

Empowering the Next Generation

Girls in ICT Day is a global initiative that inspires and encourages young women to explore careers in technology. It brings together students, professionals, and mentors to share knowledge, experiences, and opportunities in the ICT field.

Building Skills for the Future

Through workshops, coding sessions, and mentorship programs, girls gain practical digital skills that prepare them for future careers in software development, cybersecurity, data science, and more.

Inspiring Collaboration & Innovation

Girls collaborate on real-world projects, build confidence, and learn how technology can solve problems in their communities. It’s not just about coding—it’s about creating impact.
